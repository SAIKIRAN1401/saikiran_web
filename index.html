BLACK SPACE

description

An ordinary morning at a small-town High School turns into a nightmare when anonymous figures in masks have committed a massacre leaving four dead students.

 episodes

EPISODE-1
The plot of the series revolves around a school shooting plunging an entire high school (its teachers, its students and their parents) into shock. Throughout the episodes, the series also addresses the themes of school bullying , conflicting relationships between adolescents and their parents as well as the ever-increasing influence of the internet and new technologies on the behavior of young people.

 
EPISODE-2
What's the Story? BLACK SPACE is an Israeli thriller about a police detective who must rely on unorthodox means to investigate a deadly mass shooting at a local high school.
